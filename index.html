Person Do Thing is a game for 2 or more people.

In each round a Clue Giver generates a new secret word and tries to get the other players to guess that word.

But the Clue Giver can only describe the secret word using the 36 words in the box above.

The other players can ask as many questions as they'd like, but the Clue Giver can answer using only the words in the box. For an additional challenge, require the other players to also use only the words in the box to ask questions.

For example, say the secret word is "intelligence". The Clue Giver might say something like "Person have thing, do good think. Person not have thing, do no good think".
